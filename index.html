FAQ

Q: How to determine which food in the supermarket would be turned into Pure Purée?

In supermarkets, the staffs or stocks management would review their fresh food stock everyday to determine, which produce to put into the Pure Purée making machine.

Q: How to ensure safety of Pure Purée?

The food paste’s consistency and nutrient content will be measured and monitored. An appropriate amount of natural preservatives is added for longer life-shelf storage.

Q: Why the is Pure Purée ideal for 3D food printing and other creative food reconstructions?

Pure Purée machine would ensure the food material used has a small particle size. This can create shear-thinning food inks and favourable for 3D printing and further creative reconstruction use.
